## The Challenge

When patients are treated across multiple sectors and specialties, no single clinician has the full picture of their medication. That leads to errors — wrong dosages, harmful interactions, duplicate prescriptions. FMB is a national, evidence-based decision support system designed to help clinicians identify and prevent medication errors in real time.

## My Role

Design Lead — product vision and strategy, user research, prototyping, and user testing.

## Outcome

Improved the existing live product and developed product vision and strategy for two new product areas: a BI tool for analytics and an expansion into paediatrics.

---

## Strengthening what's live

FMB was already in production across regional medicine systems and GP practices when I joined. The first priority was improving the existing product — understanding how clinicians were actually using it, where it fell short, and what would make the biggest difference in their daily workflow.

That meant research with real users in real clinical settings, followed by iterative design improvements that could be shipped without disrupting the workflows people already relied on.

## Expanding the vision

Beyond improving the core product, we developed the vision and strategy for two new directions. First, a BI tool that would give stakeholders insight into how FMB was being used and where medication errors were most common. Second, an expansion into paediatrics — applying FMB's decision support to a clinical area with very different dosing logic and safety considerations.

Both required extensive market research, stakeholder alignment, and concept development to build a credible case for investment and development.
