How I work

A process built for clarity

Great design is the result of a disciplined, collaborative process — not happy accidents.

01
Discover
Deep-dive into user research, competitive analysis, and stakeholder interviews to align on the real problem.
02
Define
Synthesise findings into clear problem statements, user journeys, and success metrics everyone agrees on.
03
Design
Rapid ideation, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes tested with real users at every iteration.
04
Deliver
Polished specs, design tokens, and close collaboration with engineers until the product ships perfectly.

About me

Designer, systems thinker, and occasional ceramicist

I'm Frederik — a product designer based in Copenhagen with six years of experience turning fuzzy briefs into polished, shipped products. I've worked with early-stage startups, Series B companies, and in-house design teams across fintech, productivity, and consumer goods.

My superpower is bridging the gap between user needs and business outcomes. I speak both fluent designer and developer, which means hand-offs are actually smooth.
